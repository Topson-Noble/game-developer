Project Details

SORTRIS is a game where you take the role of an interior designer but with a twist, the furniture falls into place! Help out five clients in designing their dream rooms and organize their stuff however you want. Get creative and try your best to give these clients the room they deserve in this cute but hectic experience!

Sortris was created by a team of five for the Games Now!'s #17 Game Jam 2025. The theme for this game jam is CUTE. This game is a collaboration of game developers between different nationalities: Pakistan, Indonesia, Nigeria, and the Philippines.

For full disclosure, this game does not contain any content derived or produced by generative AI tools. It does not feature mature content and/or themes, and all copyrighted material is credited in the documentation.

Development Details

- Engine: Unreal Engine 5.5.4

- Development Focus: Grid-style movement, physics-collision enabled, in-game snapshot mechanics.

- Platforms: PC (Windows), with potential scalability for web or mobile builds.
