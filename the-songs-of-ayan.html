Project Overview

The Songs of Ayan is a short narrative rhythm game where every beat carries meaning and every rhythm challenge has a significance.

Gameplay Story Context

, The Songs of Ayan, follows Sayo, a young man of the Ayan lineage, as he undertakes shrine-based rhythm challenges, collects drum artifacts, and seeks the blessings of Ayan Agalu, the Yoruba deity of drumming, in order to complete his initiation into the Ayan drumming guild.

The game translates real-life drumming practices into interactive mechanics, embedding cultural meaning directly into gameplay while reinforcing narrative progression.
